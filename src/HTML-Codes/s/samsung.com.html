Performance

ALL-DAY INTELLIGENT BATTERY

A full charge in the morning takes you into the night

Galaxy Note20 and Note20 Ultra are equipped with 4300mAh (typical) and 4500mAh (typical) intelligent batteries that use their power efficiently, so they last through the workday and your late-night fun.15, 16 Now, you don't just have world-class technology in your hands — you've also got the juice to make it last.

SUPER FAST CHARGING

More than 50% charge in 30 minutes17

As if an all-day battery wasn't enough, Galaxy Note20 and Note20 Ultra let you plug in to power up fast with the 25W Super Fast Charger included in the box.18 So when you do run low, it's not for long.

EASY SHARING

The power of proximity

Advanced spatial awareness technology delivers higher accuracy and a more intuitive experience, making it simpler and faster to share photos and videos with your friends. Point to Share lets you simply point your phone at a friend to share files.19
